    Typically every potential Armed Robbery target Bank, Service Station, Liquor Store, corner Shop has an alarm with a ‘panic button’ (usually silent) monitored by a Central Monitoring Centre. Once pushed Alarm Monitoring centre will then pass this activation onto the Police via a landline call to the nearest Police Communication centre who may then attend the scene.

    Panic button confusion – why was it pushed ?

    • Accidentally by staff member ?
    • Is it a false alarm ?
    • Is it a Robbery ?
    • Is it a fight or dis-order ?
    • Are firearms or weapons involved ?
    • Is it a drunk, drugged, aggressive or mentally impaired customer intimidating staff ?
    • Are the staff in a position to get to the panic button and safely press it ?
    • Has the offender already fled the scene ?

    Our Artificial Intelligence technology will eliminate this confusion by recognising and confirming the use of a firearm or other weapons within 3 seconds of the offender presenting them confirming an armed robbery is in progress. We also now have a description of the offender and the weapon.

    Our AI technology can immediately send these critical CCTV image alerts – time/date stamped with location details to Alarm Monitoring Centres, Security Guards, First Responders, CCTV Control Room operators, Police Services of a crime in progress while the criminal is still onsite and all without the criminal’s knowledge.
