Realtime Alerts

Video Management Software (VMS)

When integrated with your onsite CCTV Video Management System (VMS) receive immediate critical image alerts on screen confirming the presence of an offender with a firearm or other weapon in real time all without the offender knowing. This buys precious time to safely initiate lockdowns or emergency evacuation routes to a confirmed incident whilst waiting for Police to arrive.

Critical alerts can also be sent simultaneously to onsite Security Teams, Alarm Monitoring centres, Mobile Guards or other First Responders via SMS & email to smart devices.

Stand-alone CCTV

When you operate a stand-alone CCTV system without a Video Management System receive immediate critical SMS & email image alerts confirming the presence of an offender with a firearm or other weapon. Alerts are time, date & location stamped and can be sent to Alarm Monitoring Centres, onsite Security Teams, First Responders, Mobile Guards or you the business owner on your smart device. These alerts will be sent without the offender knowing the system has been triggered enabling a response to a confirmed incident.
